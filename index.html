What is the Mystery Gang?

The Mystery Gang is actually known as Mystery Incorporated. The group includes five characters: Scooby-Doo, Daphne Blake, Norville "Shaggy" Rogers, Velma Dinkley, and Fred Jones.

What do they do?

The Mystery Gang focuses their efforts on stopping bad guys and foiling plots. They have a funny tendency of ruining the plans of really angry and crazy people who dress up as monsters in order to scare those who have hurt them.
